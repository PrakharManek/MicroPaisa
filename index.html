The Challenge for Gen Z: Lost Value in the Digital Age

Gen Z in India is digitally active but often struggles with building consistent savings and investment habits

A large value of digital money is lost unnoticed as users remain unaware of their small, frequent transaction spends

Current investment products can be complex, intimidating, and feel inaccessible for many young users

There is no existing solution that seamlessly turns everyday spending into automatic savings to build wealth gradually

Our Solution: Automated Round-Off Micro-Investing

How It Works

Every payment you make triggers two simple transactions: one to the merchant, and the rounded-off spare change goes directly into your savings account

Your savings are then automatically pooled and invested through our trusted partnerships with established investment agencies and brokers

Ready for India's Digital Economy & Regulatory Landscape

Scalable Model

Our model is designed for a phased rollout, starting with urban Gen Z and expanding to wider demographics across India

Built on UPI

We are built on India's robust UPI and digital payment ecosystem, which processes over 20 billion transactions monthly

Fully Compliant

We strictly comply with RBI's payments bank regulations and all SEBI requirements for investment facilitation to ensure your money is safe

Trusted Partnerships

Partnerships with established investment agencies ensure a smooth and trusted investment flow for your savings

Transparent & Simple Pricing

User Subscription

A simple, fixed subscription fee based on the savings plan you choose

Withdrawal Fee

Partner Commissions

The platform also earns revenue through brokerage commissions and revenue shares from our investment partners
